## Day 0-1: Welcome & Brand Discovery

### What the Client Sees/Does

**Day 0 (First 30 minutes)**
1. **Signup** â€” Creates account, enters email/password
2. **Business Essentials** â€” Enters business name, website URL, selects industry
3. **AI Brand Scan** â€” Watches AI extract brand elements from their website
4. **Brand Snapshot Review** â€” Reviews auto-generated Brand Guide:
   - Colors (3-6 extracted from website)
   - Logo (if detected)
   - Tone keywords (Professional, Friendly, Bold, etc.)
   - Voice description
   - Audience overview
5. **Weekly Focus Selection** â€” Chooses content focus (Awareness, Engagement, Conversion)

## Days 2-3: Content Review & First Approval

### What the Client Sees/Does

**Day 2**
- Logs in to review generated content more carefully
- Previews each of the 7 content items
- Edits any content that needs adjustment:
  - Updates copy if tone is slightly off
  - Swaps images if needed
  - Adjusts scheduled times
- Submits first content item for approval (if workflow enabled)

**Day 3**
- Navigates to Approvals queue
- Reviews first content item with:
  - Full preview
  - Brand Fidelity Score (BFS)
  - Compliance status
- Approves first content item
- Sees it move to "Scheduled" status on calendar

### What Success Team Does

- [ ] **Day 2:** If client hasn't logged in since Day 0, send "Your content is waiting" email
- [ ] **Day 2:** Monitor for clients who generated content but never reviewed
- [ ] **Day 3:** If no approvals yet, send "Ready to approve?" email with direct link to Approvals
- [ ] **Day 3:** For trial users, remind them of trial limits (2 posts) and timeline

### Success Criteria for Days 2-3

| Metric | Target | Status |

## Week 2: Building Content Rhythm

### What the Client Sees/Does

**Week 2 Overview**
- Establishes regular login habit (2-3x per week)
- Generates new content beyond initial 7-day plan
- Uses Creative Studio to customize designs
- Refines Brand Guide based on early results:
  - Adjusts tone if content was slightly off
  - Updates keywords to better match brand voice
  - Adds photography style rules ("No stock photos of people")
- Approves and schedules content for Week 3

**Key Activities**
- [ ] Generate Week 2 content plan
- [ ] Explore Creative Studio templates
- [ ] Upload brand-specific images to Library
- [ ] Connect additional social platforms (if not already done)
- [ ] Review any early performance data

### What Success Team Does

- [ ] **Week 2 Start:** Check if client has generated new content beyond initial 7-day plan
- [ ] **Week 2 Mid:** If client hasn't returned since Week 1, send re-engagement email
- [ ] **Week 2 End:** Schedule 15-min check-in call (optional for high-touch accounts)

## Week 4: Analytics Review & 30-Day Checkpoint

### What the Client Sees/Does

**Week 4 Overview**
- Receives 30-day summary (automated or from Success team)
- Reviews overall performance metrics:
  - Total posts published
  - Engagement metrics (if available)
  - Brand Fidelity Score trends
- Provides feedback on experience
- Discusses expansion opportunities (more platforms, more content)

**Key Activities**
- [ ] Complete 30-day analytics review
- [ ] Participate in 30-day check-in call (if scheduled)
- [ ] Provide feedback on onboarding and product experience
- [ ] Confirm ongoing content strategy
- [ ] Upgrade from trial (if applicable)

### What Success Team Does

- [ ] **Week 4 Start:** Send 30-day milestone email with summary stats
- [ ] **Week 4 Mid:** Schedule 30-day check-in call (recommended for all accounts)
- [ ] **Week 4 End:** Complete 30-day success review
- [ ] **Week 4 End:** For trial users, send final upgrade offer

### 30-Day Check-In Call Agenda

1. **Celebrate Wins** (5 min)
   - Acknowledge content published
   - Highlight any engagement wins
   
2. **Review Experience** (10 min)
   - How was onboarding?
   - Is the AI capturing your brand voice?
   - What's working? What's challenging?
   
3. **Optimize Setup** (10 min)
   - Brand Guide refinements
   - Platform connections
   - Content strategy adjustments
   
4. **Next Steps** (5 min)
   - Upcoming features
   - Expansion opportunities
   - Upgrade discussion (for trial users)

| Metric | Target | Status |

| 15+ total posts published | Yes | â˜ |
| Client is "satisfied" or "very satisfied" | Yes | â˜ |
