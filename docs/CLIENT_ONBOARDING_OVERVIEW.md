## What Is Client Onboarding?

Client onboarding is the process of taking a new user from initial signup through their first successful content publication. The goal is to deliver **"first value"** as quickly as possibleâ€”typically within the first 7 days.

**First Value Definition:** The client has published at least one piece of on-brand content through POSTD.

## Journey Stages

### Stage 1: Discovery & Signup
**Duration:** 1-5 minutes  
**Entry Points:**
- Direct signup (`/signup`)
- Trial signup (`/signup?trial=7`)
- Referral/invite link

**What Happens:**
- Account created
- Email verified (if required)
- User enters onboarding flow

### Stage 2: Brand Discovery
**Duration:** 5-10 minutes  
**Onboarding Steps:** 2-5

**What Happens:**
1. **Business Essentials** â€” Name, website, industry captured
2. **AI Website Scrape** â€” Brand elements extracted automatically:
   - Logo and images (10-15 max)
   - Color palette (6 colors)
   - Voice and tone analysis
   - Key messaging and offerings
3. **Brand Guide Review** â€” Client reviews and refines extracted brand profile

**Key Deliverable:** Complete Brand Guide (the "Brand Brain" for all AI agents)

### Stage 3: Content Generation
**Duration:** 2-5 minutes  
**Onboarding Steps:** 6-7

**What Happens:**
1. **Weekly Focus Selection** â€” Client chooses content strategy
2. **7-Day Content Plan** â€” AI generates personalized content package:
   - 5 social media posts
   - 1 email newsletter
   - 1 Google Business Profile post

**Key Deliverable:** 7-day content calendar ready for review

### Stage 4: Platform Connection & First Publish
**Duration:** 5-15 minutes (varies by platforms)  
**Onboarding Steps:** 8-9

**What Happens:**
1. **Calendar Preview** â€” Client reviews and adjusts content schedule
2. **Platform Connection** â€” Client connects social accounts (OAuth flow)
3. **First Approval** â€” Client approves first content item
4. **First Publish** â€” Content publishes at scheduled time

**Key Deliverable:** First published content (First Value achieved!)

### Stage 5: Ongoing Engagement
**Duration:** Days 7-30+  
**Post-Onboarding**

**What Happens:**
1. **Content Rhythm** â€” Regular content generation and publishing
2. **Brand Guide Refinement** â€” Ongoing optimization based on performance
3. **Feature Exploration** â€” Creative Studio, Analytics, Brand Intelligence
4. **30-Day Review** â€” Success checkpoint and optimization

**Key Deliverable:** Established content workflow

**Criteria:**

### Milestone 2: Platform Connected âœ“
**Criteria:**

- Platform verified and ready for publishing

### Milestone 3: First Value Achieved âœ“
**Criteria:**

- Client has verified published content on platform

### Milestone 4: 30-Day Success âœ“
**Criteria:**
- 15+ posts published

- Brand Guide refined at least once
- Client reports satisfaction with product

## Handoff Points

### Sales â†’ Success
**When:** After signed contract or trial signup  
**What's Handed Off:**
- Client company name and contact info
- Industry and business type
- Client goals and pain points
- Expected posting frequency
- Preferred platforms

**How:** CRM handoff, kickoff meeting invitation

### Onboarding â†’ Ongoing Success
**When:** After first publish (First Value achieved)  
**What's Handed Off:**
- Onboarding completion status
- Brand Guide summary
- Platforms connected
- Content published
- Any issues encountered during onboarding

**How:** Automated status update, success dashboard

### Success â†’ Support
**When:** Technical issues or escalations  
**What's Handed Off:**
- Client context and history
- Specific issue description
- Steps already attempted
- Urgency level

**How:** Support ticket with full context

## Common Issues & Resolutions

| Issue | Cause | Resolution |
|-------|-------|------------|
| Website scrape fails | Site blocks bots, complex SPA, or offline | Offer manual Brand Guide setup |
| No colors extracted | Single-color site or image-heavy | Manual color picker entry |
| Content sounds generic | Incomplete Brand Guide | Refine tone keywords and voice description |
| Publishing fails | OAuth expired or permissions issue | Re-connect platform, verify permissions |
| Client drops off after onboarding | Overwhelmed by features | Send focused "next step" guidance |
